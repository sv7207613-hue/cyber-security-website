Cyber Security in Real Life

Learn how cyber attacks happen in daily life and how you can protect yourself from digital threats.

Common Cyber Attacks

Phishing

Fake emails or messages that trick users into sharing passwords or bank details.

Malware

Harmful software that damages systems or steals sensitive data.

Ransomware

Locks your data and demands payment to restore access.

How to Stay Safe Online

Strong Passwords

Use unique and strong passwords for every account.

Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security to your accounts.

Update Software

Keep your apps and system updated to avoid vulnerabilities.
